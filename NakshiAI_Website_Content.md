```
The bot asks you three things:

· What is your shop or business name?
· Which city are you in?

**STEP 4 — Send Your First Jewelry Photo**
```
Take any photo of any jewelry piece — ring, necklace, earring, bangle, maang tikka, 
kada, anything — and send it directly in the chat.

Photo quality tips (shown in bot):
· Natural daylight gives the best results
· Place jewelry on a plain surface (white cloth, dark velvet)
· Shoot from directly above or at a 45° angle
· No blurry or very dark photos
```

**STEP 5 — Choose Your Options (Buttons Only)**
```
The bot shows you buttons. You tap. No typing required.

Choose:
· Model type (options vary by plan)
· Background style (studio white, lifestyle, plain)
· Gemstone color (if applicable)
· Metal finish (gold / rose gold / silver / white gold)

Tap confirm. Processing begins.
```

**STEP 6 — Receive Your Model Shot in 60 Seconds**
```
Within 60 seconds (usually faster), the same jewelry piece appears on a 
professional model — photorealistic, well-lit, ready to share.

The result lands in the same WhatsApp chat. 
Download it with one tap.
Send it to your customer. Post it on Instagram. Add it to your catalog.
